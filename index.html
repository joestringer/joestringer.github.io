Profile

I'm a recent graduate of the University of Waikato in Hamilton, New Zealand. The focus of my degree was in Computer Science with a specialisation in Networks. My work on Open vSwitch was recognised with first class honours.

My ongoing contributions to Open Source include:

  • Arch Linux - Assistance with the Wiki and PKGBUILDs
  • RouteFlow - Development on extensibility and use with OpenFlow hardware
  • Open vSwitch - OpenFlow-1.1+ feature implementation

You'll find me hanging around FOSS mailing lists, Hacker News and Slashdot. In my spare time, I enjoy listening to music and jamming on my drum kit.
